Hello! My name is Anna Clara. I am from Brazil, but I am studying in Savannah,
Georgia. I have been an international student since 2019. I started learning English as a second language in Massachusetts, and soon after, I earned my associate degree in ART. In 2023, I transferred my credits to the Savannah College of Art and Design. I am in my final year, studying Illustration with Surface Design as my minor.

Living in a world where my first language differs from others gave me the idea of finding common ground. Art is a universal language. Anyone from any corner of the world can understand an image or message; that is why I use my art to express my way of thinking and seeing the world. I share everyday issues that I truly believe humanity can solve together. My illustrations present questions, solutions, or often both. Through my art, I provide everything that can encourage children's development and help parents raise better human beings for the future.

My illustrations aim to convey hope for better days and spread positive energy.
